ABOUT US

A JAM PRODUCTION, Inc is an artist development and product/service company celebrating 45 years in the music, film, video and TV production industry.

Beginning in 1971, from a small office located just south of Atlanta, Georgia, JAM began booking and managing musical groups throughout the southeast. As the agency grew, JAM booked not only local but national and international artists throughout the entire world.

As JAM's artist roster grew so did its need to outfit equipment for its artists, so JAM Music was founded.

Since that time, JAM Music has grown its service offereing in many areas including sound and music equipment rentals, production and lighting rentals, staging and trussing rentals, as well as video wall rentals. At JAM, we pride ourselves on the consistency of our value and the authenticity of our service.

Since relocating our offices to the greater Nashville area, we have spent the last 5 years establishing ourselves as an industry leader for both music entertainment/production rentals and sales alike. Our product and service offering includes consultation and fulfillment for all touring, festival, wedding, corporate and individual productions. At JAM, we believe the success of our company begins with the intergity of our promis and the consistency of our service.
